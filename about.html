A brief history

Commenced trading from Coventry in 1982 on the back ofthe growing market for PVCu windows, doors and later conservatories; installing throughout the entire Midlands.

Our conservatories, are designed with quality in mind in to last for at least 30 years, with proper groundwork ,insulated bases with well matched , high quality brickwork; highly insulated, often as good or better than your house, but with a glass or highly insulated polycarbonate and brick or panelled walls with underfloorheating, lighting and power.

We will always treat you,the customer with respect,and courtesy. We will leave you to decide if our suggesti ons for cost effective, tasteful ideas are within your budget. We will never take up your time in the hope that sitting it out will obtain us an order.

Our conservatories are often superior in quality to the national companies and at a much lower price as we as we do not employ free lance sales staff and charge no sales commissions-just high quality and fair prices together with sound advice from 30 plus years of trading.




Styles vary as they are all bespoke; names that you will recognise include Orangeries, Edwardian, Victorian, garden rooms, lean to`s loggias and skypod roofs.

Our windows are designed expertly, to complement the home installed to the very best standards with the highest levels of insulation if required and are currently lasting over 35 years. Ask to see the 1980`s installations that are still looking well. .

We always use the best products available and install them to the best standards .

Quotations and designs are free; we offer PVCu, aluminium and hardwood in all the latest colours i.e. white,cream, green, various timber shades,black and grey; together with the best insulating,long life A rated sealed units.
